Rubber Dam

Flexible water retaining structures used for ponding for ground water recharging as a weir or to divert river water to canals for irrigation and power purposes.

Bituminous Geomembrane

Advanced multicomponent waterproofing membrane specially designed to be stronger, more watertight and more durable than resin-based geomembranes.

More Details

Concrete Mattress

Perfect symbiosis of geotextiles and concrete custom-design to suit any geometry, with a range of constant mattress thickness even on uneven surfaces.

More Details

Dam Leak Investigation

Magnetic resonance imaging technology created specifically to find subsurface water pathways that can be adjusted in real-time and is adaptable to every environment.

More Details

8ARS Gate

Quick, non-intrusive technology used to identify, map and model groundwater flow & seepage paths, similar to algorithms that enable medical professionals to “see” blood vessels inside the human body.

More Details

Pocket Dam

Portable water barrier – a perfect solution for creating a barrier for cleaning riverbanks, performing aquatic construction, removing pollution and controlling liquid spills.

More Details

TUFFBOOM

Water Ballast barriers designed for debris control and public dam safety.They are commonly used to protect dams from debris and to keep boaters safe and out of spillways and hydro-electric water intakes.
